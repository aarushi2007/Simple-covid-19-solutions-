What is covid-19?

Scientists discovered a disease called covid-19 during the year 2020. It is a highly malignant disease which had originated from another infected animal before it infected humans. The intermediary animal is still a mystery however some scientists suspect it's likely a scaly mammal called a pangolin. Most importantly, scientists are unable to figure out how the mammal infected humans. The effects of covid_19 on a human begin is dreadful; for example, common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, cough, fatigue, breathing difficulties, and loss of smell and taste.
